Plantation Shutters

No other window coverings add style and class like plantation shutters. Plantation shutters can fit in features that others can't, whether it's an odd-shaped window, bay or box windows, or sliding doors. Their unique ability to be hinged, bi-fold or sliding gives them flexibility to suit any home.

Roman Blinds

Good looking down or up, roman blinds are perfect for hiding windows or other openings. They provide a softer and cosier look than other window fitments without taking up room like curtains do.

Venetian Blinds

Timeless and practical, venetians can allow light through whilst maintaining privacy as well as quickly disappear. Whether you're after wood, PVC, or aluminium venetians, we have an entire range in sizes ranging from 25mm all the way up to 63mm slats.

Vertical Blinds

Affordable, practical and strong. Vertical blinds can be used for both windows and doors due to their horizontal movement. Unlike most other blind types, verticals are suitable for wet areas such as kitchens and bathrooms. They come in a range of blockout fabric types as well as insulating PVC.
